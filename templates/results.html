How Carbon Stock Changes and Credits are Calculated:

Carbon Credits and Carbon stock changes (tC) are calculated by assuming all shrubland, grassland, and cropland are converted to tree cover, with other land covers unchanged. The baseline is the sum of the areas of land covers to be converted multiplied by their average carbon stocks per hectare.

Annual Carbon stock change is the difference between the total carbon stock of the new forest and the baseline,factoring in tree growth rates and characteristics.
